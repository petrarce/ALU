1-(000) Start condition. Enables division
2-(001) Sets data into A and B registers. Clears C and n registers.
3-(010) Shiftsh B and n registers
4-(011) Refreshs A register (A=A-B) if its nessessary. Shifts C register
5-(100) Shifts B and n registers
6-(101) Buffer condition (before 3 or 4 conditions)
